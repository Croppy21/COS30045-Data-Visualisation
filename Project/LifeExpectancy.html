Figure 3: Comparison of Life Expectancy Across Developed Nations (Source: Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development)


This bar chart compares life expectancy across developed countries. By analyzing how life expectancy changed globally during the COVID-19 pandemic, we can gain insights into the effectiveness of various public health approaches adopted by different nations.

As you can see, most countries' Life Expectancy ranges beween 62.3 and 84.6. Countries such as Australia, Japan, and Korea were considered the highest on the ranking, and South Afria at the lowest. Small differences can be seen between the years but majority of counties' Life Expectancy went down.

For interactivity, you can hover over each bar for more information regarding the country and Life Expectancy age.
