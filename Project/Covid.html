Figure 2: The Increase of Deaths Due to COVID-19 by Year and Month of Occurrence: (source: Australian Bureau of Statistics)


This line graph tracks the impact of COVID-19 on Australian mortality over time. By analyzing trends, potential infection waves, and changes in death rates, we can assess the effectiveness of public health measures and inform future strategies to mitigate future outbreaks.

As you can see, the number of cases has dramatically Increased between the years, with 2022 having the highest amount of deaths in most months. The blue line represents 2020, The green represents 2021, and the red line represents 2022.

For interactivity, you can hover over each data point to getter a better view of the data, as well as toggle which years can be shown/hidden on the chart. It might help differentiating the closer numbers.
